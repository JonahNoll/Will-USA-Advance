# Will-USA-Advance
This calculator will be able to answer whether or not the USA will advance to the WBC QF. This tool will come in handy in case the game goes to extra innings and to answer what scenarios the US not only advances, but wins the group.

As of 3/11/26 the US advancement is dependent on the results of the Italy v Mexico game at 7pm EST.

If Italy wins then there is nothing to calculate. Italy will win the group and the US will advance as the second place finisher.
HOWEVER
If Mexico wins (which is expected as they are the heavy favorites) then there will be a 3-way tie

Who wins the group and who advances will be based on something called the RUNS QUOTIENT which is how many runs a team allowed per out

Thats where this calculator will come in handy. By providing the runs scored for both Italy and Mexico as well as how many innings the game goes, this script can calculate and display who advances and in what order.
